About GradVoyage

GradVoyage is more than a consultancy — it’s a personalized journey designed to help students and professionals present their best selves to the world’s most prestigious universities.

We specialize in crafting authentic Statements of Purpose (SOPs), persuasive Letters of Recommendation (LORs), and globally relevant Resumes that align with your academic ambitions and career goals.

Our mission is to blend creativity, strategy, and empathy — empowering applicants to turn aspirations into achievements, and dreams into global destinations.

  • ✍️ SOP Writing — Authentic, impactful statements that highlight your voice and goals.
  • 🧾 LOR Drafting — Structured, credible recommendations aligned with your achievements.
  • 📄 Resume Crafting — Tailored, global-standard resumes for graduate applications.
  • 🎯 Profile Evaluation — Insightful feedback to strengthen your academic positioning.
  • 🏫 University Shortlisting — Balanced list based on your academic fit and aspirations.
  • 🤝 End-to-End Mentorship — Complete guidance from ideation to submission.
