About GradVoyage

GradVoyage is a personalized admissions consulting studio helping students and professionals craft powerful, authentic applications for top global universities.

Founded by Swarna Rao Kambhampaty, GradVoyage blends creativity and structure to create compelling SOPs, LORs, and Resumes that truly represent who you are and what you aspire to achieve.

  • ✍️ SOP Writing — Authentic, impactful statements that highlight your voice and goals.
  • 🧾 LOR Drafting — Structured, credible recommendations aligned with your achievements.
  • 📄 Resume Crafting — Tailored, global-standard resumes for graduate applications.
  • 🎯 Profile Evaluation — Insightful feedback to strengthen your academic positioning.
  • 🏫 University Shortlisting — Balanced list based on your academic fit and aspirations.
  • 🤝 End-to-End Mentorship — Complete guidance from ideation to submission.

Meet Our Founder

With a passion for empowering global learners, Swarna Rao Kambhampaty founded GradVoyage to help students craft authentic, narrative-driven applications. Her 8+ years of HR and academic experience have helped hundreds reach their dream universities.
